Stop getting support line calls

Check what you need to do to stop getting support line calls

Steps for stopping calls is similar to setting up ready to anaswer calls

  1. Step 1 Call the setup phone number

    With the phone you've given us the number for, call this number:

    0844 770 7081

  2. Step 2 Enter your agent code

    A recorded message will ask you to enter your 'agent code'.

    We will text your 'agent code' before your shift.

    Your agent code is four digits long followed by star. For example, ‘8521*’.

  3. Step 3 Enter the agent pin

    Next you will be asked to enter 'agent pin':

    1234*

    This number is always the same.

  4. Step 4 Update your status to logged out

    Next you'll be asked to change your status, which means being available or not to answer calls.

    Change your status to logged out by:

    1. 1. Press 1 on your phone
    2. 2. You'll hear 'Your current status is logged out. To log in press 1'
    3. 3. Press 1 again to login, which changes your status to available
    4. 4. You'll then hear ‘Your status has been updated.’
    5. 5. Finish by pressing 2 or hanging up

  5. Step 5 You have stopped getting support line calls

    Now you have entered the right information in the right order, you won't get any more support line.

    1. Setup your phone ready to answer calls
