How to finish calls

Every call will be different. There is no one way to finish the call.

Check before you finish the call that you’ve covered:

  1. Introduction
  2. Caller’s name
  3. Vulnerabilities they might have
  4. Support they need
  5. Consent to share information
  6. Their location
  7. Contact details
  8. Confirm their next steps
  9. Say they can call again
