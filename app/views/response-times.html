Response times

The organisation requesting support receives an acknowledgment email within 24 hours of having received their request through the ‘Request support as a voluntary and community organisation’ service.

If the request has been made by a liaison lead, they will also receive an acknowledgment email within 24 hours, detailing the request for support they have made for the local organisation.

This does not mean arrangements for support are made in 24 hours. It is a first contact timeframe.

Urgent requests

If a request is urgent,follow Step 9. in the 'Broker role: step by step'section.
