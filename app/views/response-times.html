Response times

Information managers contact the organisation requesting support within 24 hours of having received their request through the ‘Get support as a local organisation’ service.

If the request has been made by a liaison lead, they will also receive an email within 24 hours, detailing the request for support they have made for the local organisation.

This does not mean arrangements for support are made in 24 hours. It is a first contact timeframe for information managers to acknowledge the request for support.

Urgent requests

If a request is urgent, 5 regional teams each have a duty manager available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.
