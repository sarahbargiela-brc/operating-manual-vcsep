Glossary of terms

New request

Request has been submitted through the ‘Request support as a voluntary or community organisation’ service online, but has not yet been picked up by the Liaison Lead or Information Manager.

Review

Request has been picked up by Information Manager and assigned to an area and Liaison Lead for local follow up and details checking.

Escalated to Tactical Cell

Request has been checked by Liaison Lead and needs to be brokered at the Regional Tactical Cell meeting.

Broker found

Request has had a broker assigned at the Regional Tactical Cell meeting, who now is actioning the request.

Closed

Request has been brokered and sufficient support provided to local organisation to fulfil it.

Resolved locally

Support for this request can be met locally.
