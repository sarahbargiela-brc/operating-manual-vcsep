Brokers for COVID-19 response

Brokers operate across 5 regions in England: North, Midlands and East, South West and Channel Islands, South East and London. They support requests escalated to regional, multiagency cells

To find the most up to date list of brokers for a region, go to the SharePoint list in the VCSEP MS Teams site. Click on the 3 line menu in the top left corner. Select ‘Brokers’ from the panel on the left. You will see all the brokers in the multiagency cells.

Currently each multiagency cell region includes these brokering organisations:

  • British Red Cross
  • Re:ACT
  • St John Ambulance
  • Salvation Army
  • Victim Support
  • Business in the Community
