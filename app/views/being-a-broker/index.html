Being a broker

A multiagency cell (MAC) duty manager can invite any new organisation interested in becoming a VCS EP support service broker to a cell meeting. At the meeting they will be able to experience the requests coming to the service, and so understand the types of support needed.

If after attending the MAC meeting the organisation:

  • fully understands the support the service intends to offer
  • thinks that they can contribute something useful as a broker
  • is are still interested in becoming a broker

The cell lead or project manager will send them the welcome email, which includes the information guidance training link and their honorary contract.
