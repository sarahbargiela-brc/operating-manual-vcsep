Broker role: step by step

Step 1. Complete information governance training

You will receive an email from the Multiagency Cell Lead with information governance training instructions and your honorary contract.

Complete the British Red Cross information governance training, on the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC) e-learning platform.

Access information governance training instructions



Step 2. Sign and return your honorary contract

Sign your honorary contract and email a copy to VCSEP_IM@redcross.org.uk.



Step 3. Log in to MS Teams and access SharePoint

When you have completed your information governance training and signed your honorary contract, you will be sent log in details for MS Teams and SharePoint by the VCS EP 'Request support as a voluntary or community organisation' service team.



Step 4. Complete your broker bio

In MS Teams, complete your broker bio. The template for this is in the toolbar at the top: click on ‘Broker bios’.



Step 5. In SharePoint, familiarise yourself with the request status definitions

Access SharePoint by clicking on ‘Requests’ in the top toolbar in MS Teams. This takes you to the SharePoint home page, where you can see the all the requests.
