Useful contacts for information managers

As an information manager for the 'Request support as a local organisation' service you should have received a list of all the contact information you need by email after you completed your information governance training.

Your list will include contact details for:

  • liaison leads
  • information managers
  • tactical cell duty managers
  • crisis response

If you've completed the training and have not received the contact list, or if you have any issues with how the service is working or with any processes related to the service, contact:

British Red Cross VCSEPservice@RedCross.org.uk
