Replying after reviewing requests

Send this email to the Information Manager after you have reviewed the support requests in your local area that they sent you.

Checklist

1. Copy and paste example text below into the email.

2. Replace any text in square brackets with real information.



Dear Information Manager

Re: Local review of support requests

I've reviewed the support requests you sent me from a local organisation in my area.

Can take forward locally:

  • [Name of organisation, referral date dd/mm/yy]

Many thanks,

Liaison Lead



We recommend you set up and save this example response as a draft email, and copy it from there each time you need to use it.
