Acknowledging support has been found for a local organisation

Send this email to a local organisation to let them know that support has been found for their request.

Checklist

1. To: requesting organisation

2. Cc. liaison lead, lead broker and supporting broker.

3. Bcc. VCSEP_IM@redcross.org.uk



Dear [organisation name]

Re: We have found two organisations who are able to support you.

I am writing to update you on the request made by or on behalf of [Organisation name] on [dd/mm/yy].

We have now found two organisations who will be taking forward the request for support.

Here are contact details for the organisations providing the support.

Broker organisation name:

[Lead broker organisation]

Name:

[Lead broker name]

Email:

[Supporting broker organisation]

Supporting broker name:

[Broker offer]

If you have any difficulty making contact with the broker above, contact your VCS EP support service liaison lead [liaison lead email address] straight away to request an update.

Many thanks,

Information Manager
