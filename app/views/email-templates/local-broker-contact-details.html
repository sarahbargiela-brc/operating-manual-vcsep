Sharing local broker contact details with information managers

Send this email to the Information Manager to notify them you have found local provision for a support request. We recommend you set up and save this example response as a draft email, and copy it from there each time you need to use it.

Checklist

1. Copy and paste example text below into the email.

2. Replace any text in square brackets with real information.



Dear Information Manager,

Re: A local broker has been found to provide the support

To update you on request made by [name of organisation] on [dd/mm/yy].

I have found a local broker who take forward the support request. Here are their details:

Name:

Address:

Email:

Mobile phone number:

How they can help

They told us that they would be able to support by [brief details of they have said they can provide support]

From: [dd/mm/yy]

To: [dd/mm/yy]

Your actions

  1. Add the broker to our contact list.
  2. Update the status to 'Met locally'.

Many thanks,

Liaison Lead

'Request support as a voluntary or community organisation' service
