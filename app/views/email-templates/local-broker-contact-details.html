Sharing local broker contact details with information managers

Send this email to the Information Manager to notify them you have found local provision for a support request.

Checklist

1. To: VCSEP_IM@redcross.org.uk.



Dear Information Manager,

Re: A local broker has been found to provide support

To update you on request made by [Organisation] on [dd/mm/yy].

I have found a local broker who take forward the support request. Here are their details:

Name:

Address:

Email:

Mobile phone number:

How they can help

They told us that they would be able to support by [brief details of they have said they can provide support]

From: [dd/mm/yy]

To: [dd/mm/yy]

Your actions

  1. Add the broker details to SharePoint list.
  2. Update the status to 'Closed'.

Many thanks,

[Liaison lead name]

VCS EP Request Support service
