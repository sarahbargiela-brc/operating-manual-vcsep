Acknowledging support requests

Send this email to a liaison lead or local organisation within 24 hours of receiving their request for support.

Checklist

1. Copy and paste example text below into the email.

2. Replace any text in square brackets with real information.

3. Copy and paste the request report into the email.



Dear [name of organisation]

Re: Confirmation of your request from the Request support for a voluntary or community organisation service, received on [dd/mm/yy].

Thank you for submitting a request through the VCS EP Request support as a voluntary or community organisation service. Your request will now be reviewed. Please allow 10 working days for actions to be assigned to this request.

Below are the details of your request.

Many thanks,

Information Manager

'Request support as a voluntary or community organisation' service



We recommend you set up and save this example response as a draft email, and copy it from there each time you need to use it.
