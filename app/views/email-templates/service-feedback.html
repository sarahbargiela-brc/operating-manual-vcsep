Acknowledging support has been found for a local organisation

Send this email to a local organisation to let them know that support has been found for their request. Brokers and liaison leads should be cc.ed into this email.

Checklist

1. Copy and paste example text below into the email.

2. Replace any text in square brackets with real information.

3. Copy and paste the request report into the email.



Dear [organisation name]

Re: Service feedback for VCS EP Support Service

I am writing to ask you for some feedback on a request made by or on behalf of [Organisation name] on [dd/mm/yy].

We would like to know how we handled the request for support.

Complete this short feedback form. It should take no longer than 5 minutes.

You answers will help us know which areas of the service are working well and which need development.

Many thanks,

Information Manager

'Request support as a voluntary or community organisation' service





We recommend you set up and save this example response as a draft email, and copy it from there each time you need to use it.
