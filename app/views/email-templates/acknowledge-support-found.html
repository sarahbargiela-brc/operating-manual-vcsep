Acknowledging support has been found for a local organisation

Send this email to a local organisation to let them know that support has been found for their request. Brokers and liaison leads should be cc.ed into this email. We recommend you set up and save this example response as a draft email, and copy it from there each time you need to acknowledge a new request.

Checklist

1. Copy and paste example text below into the email.

2. Replace any text in square brackets with real information.

3. Copy and paste the request report into the email.



Dear Liaison Lead

Re: We have found an organisation that is able to support you.

I am writing to update you on the request made by or on behalf of [Organisation name] on [dd/mm/yy].

We have now found an organisation who will be taking forward the request for support.

Here are contact details for the organisation providing the support.

Name:

Address:

Email:

Phone number:

They have said they can support your organisation by: [what they can do to support, for example supplying DBS-checked volunteers]

From: [dd/mm/yy]

To: [dd/mm/yy]

We will leave you to contact each other to make the necessary arrangements. The organisation has your contact details.

Many thanks,

Information Manager
