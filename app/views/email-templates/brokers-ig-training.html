Sending information governance training instructions to brokers

Send this email to brokers who need to complete our information governance training. We recommend you set up and save this example response as a draft email, and copy it from there each time you need to acknowledge a new request.

Checklist

1. Copy and paste example text below into the email.



Dear Broker

Re: Information governance training for the 'Request support as a voluntary or community organisation' service

To be sure that personally identifiable information is handled appropriately in accordance with GDPR, we require all brokers to complete a 30 minute information governance training session online.

Access step-by-step instructions for completing the information governance training.

When you complete the training, save the certificate and email it to VCSEP_IM@redcross.org.uk. Without this we cannot go forward with your support offer.
