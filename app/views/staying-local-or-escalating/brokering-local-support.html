Brokering local support

If as a liaison lead you think you can find a local broker for a support request, follow these steps:

  1. Ask in your networks whether a local broker can provide support. If no broker is available, follow steps for escalating a request.
  2. If you find a local broker, you can then signpost the local organisation requiring support to the local broker. You cannot connect the broker, to the local organisation.
  3. When a local broker has been found and has agreed to support the local organisation, use the email template to report the broker's details and how they can help , to the information manager so they can update the SharePoint record.
