Information governance training

Before anyone can access personally identifiable information from SharePoint, they must complete the Red Cross Information Governance online training. This includes:

  • community engagement managers
  • information managers
  • brokers who have agreed to take forward a support request

The training takes about 30 minutes. When completed, your certificate will be sent to you and also recorded in the system, so that you can be authorised to access records in SharePoint.

Getting started

  1. Access the Information Governance e-module on the IFRC e-learning platform: ifrc.csod.com
  2. If you are already registered, log in.
  3. If you are a new user, click on the “Register” button in the bottom left of the screen.
  4. Complete your registration details.
  5. For “You are affiliated as”, select the “Staff/Volunteer Non for Profit” option.
  6. For “Division”, search for and select: “United Kingdom”.
  7. When registered, click the log in button to go to your e-learning account.

Logging in

  1. Go to the log in page, enter your username and password.
  2. If you forget your password, use the “forgot password” button.

Finding the Information Governance course

  1. When logged in, search modules in the top right of the screen. Type in “information” to find the Information Governance training.
  2. From the displayed results select “Data Matters (Information Governance)” and click on title.
  3. On the training details page, select the “Request” button. This adds the course to your learning account.

Launching your course

  1. Access your learning account by clicking “My Current Online Training” on the right of the main screen.
  2. When you are ready to take the information governance training, click “Launch”.

Saving and sending your certificate

  1. When you’ve completed the course, choose the option to save your certificate at the end.
  2. Or go to your “Learning Passport” and filter by “Completed”. Click “View completion page”, where you should be able to download your certificate.
  3. Email your training completion certificate to VCSEP_IM@redcross.org.uk.
