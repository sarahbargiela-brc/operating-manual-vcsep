What information not to collect

Do not collect any personal information about anyone who is not the person making the request, for example do not collect the name or medical requirements of a high-risk person.

This is a service supporting local organisations. We are not collecting information on, or able to provide help to, individuals through this service. If information about individuals is submitted in the form, follow the steps described in the information governance training.
