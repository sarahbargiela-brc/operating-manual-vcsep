What support it offers

The service can connect local organisations with:

  • particular volunteers they need – for example, volunteers who are DBS checked
  • specialist skills or expertise – for example, mental health charities who can provide training in mental health support
  • relevant sources of information – for example, information on available grants for local organisations

Brokers

The request support service works with regional and national brokers: organisations who can provide different types of support.

These include:

  • St John Ambulance
  • Team Rubicon
  • Salvation Army
  • Victim Support
  • Business in the Community

We regularly update our list of brokers on the VCSEP support website.

Regional Tactical Cells

Brokers are coordinated and matched to requests by the the service's Regional Tactical Cells.

These are in 5 areas of England: North, Midlands, South West, South East and London. The support offered will depend on the area you are in. Types of support offered include:

  • Personal protective equipment
  • Funding advice
  • Specialist training
  • Guidance
  • Mental health support and wellbeing
  • Volunteers
